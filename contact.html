FAQ(╹∀◠)

How long does a commission take?

Depending on the complexity, commissions typically take 3-7 days to complete, but as soon as I receive your request I will tell you how long it will take to complete your commission.

What payment methods do you accept?

I accept PayPal and bank transfers. Payment details will be provided after your commission request is approved and when all details have been discussed.

How will I receive my traditional Commissions?

If you requested a traditional commission I will send it to you by post. The shipping fees are already included in the price of the traditional commission.

What file formats will I receive the digital commissions?

You'll receive high-resolution PNG and JPG files of your completed artwork.
