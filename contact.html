Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a commission take?

Depending on the complexity, commissions typically take 3-7 days to complete once payment is received.

What payment methods do you accept?

I accept PayPal and bank transfers. Payment details will be provided after your commission request is approved.

Can I request revisions?

Yes! Minor revisions are included at each stage of the process. Major changes after approval may incur additional fees.

What file formats will I receive?

You'll receive high-resolution PNG and JPG files of your completed artwork.
