Terms of Service

Please read these terms carefully before commissioning artwork

Personal Use Only

All commissioned artwork is for personal use only. You may use the artwork as profile pictures or banners, but you may not resell the artwork or use it for commercial purposes without explicit written permission.

Rights & Copyright

CyberDuck retains all rights to the artwork and can post it on social media and in portfolios unless specifically requested otherwise. The client does not own the copyright to the commissioned artwork.

Payment Policy

A 50% non-refundable deposit is required before work begins. The remaining balance must be paid after the clean sketch is approved and before proceeding to the final stages. Payment methods include PayPal and bank transfer.

Reposting & Credit

Clients may share the commissioned artwork on social media and personal websites but must provide proper credit to CyberDuck. A link to my Instagram (@cyber_duck51) or other social media is appreciated.

Revisions & Updates

Clients will receive at least three progress updates during the commission process:

  • Initial sketch
  • Clean sketch
  • Final half-done work
  • Finished piece

Minor revisions are included at each stage. Major changes after approval of a stage may incur additional fees.

Refund Policy

The initial 50% deposit is non-refundable as it covers the time spent on sketches and planning. Once the work is halfway completed, refunds are not possible. If you need to cancel a commission, please contact me as soon as possible.

Content Restrictions

I reserve the right to decline any commission request that I feel uncomfortable with or that violates my personal boundaries. Please inquire if you're unsure about your request.
